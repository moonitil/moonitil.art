Commissions

Accepted Types of Commissions

Before we even begin discussing mediums and pricing, I feel it's important to make clear what I will and won't draw. Firstly, I'm an artist who specializes in drawing people. If you're looking for someone to illustrate a landscape, I'm not that person. Although I have done realistic drawings of people before, I'm most experienced in anime-style depictions of people - so I'm more likely to accept those commissions.

I absolutely WILL NOT draw anything offensive, derogatory, or discriminatory. If you're unsure whether or not your request falls into any of those categories, just ask me and I'll clear it up for you. If I decline to draw your request, please respect my decision. Don't try to convince me to draw it. A no is a no.

Lastly, I am entirely comfortable with drawing artwork for your project, whether that be a video game, book or album cover, etc. Each piece will be charged separately. The only thing I ask is that if you use my illustration for a project, you credit me in your final work as the artist.

Mediums

I am capable in many mediums, which include digital, alcohol marker, colored pencil, watercolor, and pencil shaded art. I am willing to complete commissions in any of these mediums.

Pricing

Pricing will vary depending on the content of the illustration. Each person or character in an image will be charged separately. For traditional pieces, the physical size of the artwork will factor into its cost. In the instance of commissioning multiple pieces of art for a project, each piece will be priced separately. Another factor in pricing is the detail of the piece - something that will need to be specified. Below in my contact info is a list of questions that if answered will make the pricing process much quicker and easier.

Contact

Questions

Feel free to DM me the answers to these questions, then we can begin discussing pricing. I'll try my best to respond within the same day! If you have any further questions, do not hesistate to ask.

  • What is the subject matter you are commissioning?
  • How many pieces are you commissioning?
  • How many characters are in each piece?
  • How detailed is each piece?
  • Which medium/s would you like the piece/s to be in?
  • Can you send me any references? (If it's a portrait of a real person, I need to know what they look like.)
  • Which style is the commission in?
  • What is the purpose of the commission?

About

I'm a self-taught artist who's been drawing ever since I can remember. About a decade ago, I began taking my artwork more seriously and began developing my style. Around this time, I had an artistic friend who introduced me to a cartoony style, and my love of drawing stylized people was born. In the following years, I honed this style and gathered influences from online sources, including multiple artists on YouTube.

My style is heavily influenced by the style in which Japanese cartoons and comics are drawn. Known as Anime or Manga, this style is characterized by delicate lines, large eyes, and fluffy hair. Many of my pieces are fan art of these Japanese cartoons.

Creating fan art is something I enjoy greatly to express my love of these series. However, I also enjoy designing and drawing my characters and making stylized portraits of my friends. One of my favorite exercises is to take a real photo of a person and draw it in my style - as if that person was from an Anime.
